Lego Galore

By Lego Enthusiast Zachary Kuta

Fun Facts

  • The Plural of Lego is Lego.
  • Lego is made of ABS plastic to ensure they never decompose.
  • Lego appreciates in value more than gold.
  • A single Lego brick can withstand up to 4,240 Newtons of Force.

The Beginning

    Ole Christiansen first created Lego in 1932. It started out as wooden toys, such as cars and piggy banks. The first iconic Lego brick was invented in later in 1949, and led to limitless possibilities for building and creativity. Ultimately leading to what the company is today, a multi-billion dollar company. They have many themes and subthemes from many beloved movies and tv shows. Their motto is "only the best is good enough." Leading them to create a large variety of pieces and designs. Lego now has captured the love of adults in addition to children. They welcome adults, and market certain products to them. Even having 18 and older sets with thousands of pieces that sell for hundreds of dollars. Lego is loved by many, and has become the most well known building brick brand out there.
