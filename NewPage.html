  1. Get Legos
    • Have your old legos still stored in the attic? You're half way there!
    • Legos are expensive, ask friends or family for their old one's or shop deals online and at garage sells.
    • Be rich. Just buy legos online for the retail price. Don't be like the other chumps.
  2. Get a Phone and be Creative
    • Any camera will do.
    • Dowload Stop Motion Studio Pro on your phone.
    • Take thousands of pictures of your legos, while moving them in small increments to make them appear as if they are moving.
  3. Post it on Youtube
    • Just do it! Fight the fear. You can't be famous if you don't try.
    • Sit back and watch the views roll in. You may only get a couple at first, but don't be discouraged.
    • Repeat the process. Repetition is the key to success.
